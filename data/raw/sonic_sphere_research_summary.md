## Key Data Caveats

### Confidence Distribution
- **High confidence (4-5/5):** ~70 artists with verified Spotify + touring data
- **Medium confidence (3/5):** ~25 artists with partial data
- **Low confidence (1-2/5):** ~15 artists (sound art, deceased, or underground)
- **Unknown (N/A):** 2 artists (Ian Dearden, Justin Gray — could not identify)

### Notable Limitations
1. **Spotify data is primary streaming source** — Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music not included due to data unavailability
2. **Social media counts are snapshots** — Follower counts fluctuate; some artists deleted accounts (Thom Yorke, Radiohead)
3. **Tour gross estimates are incomplete** — Many artists don't publicly report grosses
4. **Sound/visual artists lack standard metrics** — Tarik Barri, Christian Marclay, etc. operate outside commercial music platforms
5. **Some artists listed are deceased** — Karlheinz Stockhausen (d. 2007), The Beatles (disbanded), Talking Heads (disbanded)

### Disambiguation Issues
- **Aphex Twin:** Multiple Spotify profiles (user-uploaded content vs. official)
- **Carl Craig:** Multiple Spotify profiles
- **Monolake/Robert Henke:** Single artist with two names
- **Romy:** Solo career + member of The xx
- **Thom Yorke:** Solo + Radiohead + The Smile

## Normalization Methodology
- **Log transform:** log10(raw_value + 1) applied to Spotify ML, Social Followers, Tour Gross, RIAA Units
- **Percentile rank:** 0–100 scale within each dimension
- **Composite score:** Weighted average (Spotify 35%, Social 25%, Tour 25%, RIAA 15%)
- **Tiers:** ★ (Niche) | ★★ (Small Venue) | ★★★ (Club/Festival) | ★★★★ (Theater/Festival Headliner) | ★★★★★ (Stadium/Arena)
